Campaign Milestones represent the concrete steps the assembly follow to achieve the goal of the campaign. Use the timeline slider below to setup starting dates and durations for each milestone.

  1. Each circle represents a milestone. Slide them in the timeline to change their starting dates.
  2. The separation between circles represents the duration.
  3. Below the timeline, each circle is described by number, with its corresponding starting date and duration according to their positions in the timeline. 4. Milestones are organized within each component used in the campaign, as shown in the summary.
